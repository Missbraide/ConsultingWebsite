Frequently Asked Questions

How do you determine the exact price within each range?

Our pricing is based on the complexity and scope of your specific project. We'll assess factors such as your business size, current situation, project requirements, and timeline to provide you with a precise quote within the listed ranges.

Do you offer discounts for bundled services?

Yes! We offer package discounts when you combine multiple services. For example, combining Website Creation with Brand Identity typically results in a 10-15% savings compared to purchasing them separately.

What's your payment structure?

For one-time projects, we typically require a 50% deposit to begin work with the remaining balance due upon completion. For monthly retainers, we bill at the beginning of each month. We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal.

Do you offer a satisfaction guarantee?

Absolutely! Your satisfaction is our top priority. We work closely with you throughout the project to ensure we're meeting your expectations. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll make necessary revisions until you're happy with the outcome.
